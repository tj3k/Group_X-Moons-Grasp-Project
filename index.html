Welcome to the website about our game, Moon's Grasp.
A level-based driving game where you utilise your fuel until you reach the finishing area.


Synopsis

Stranded on the moon, with only your low oxygen tank and a simple transport vehicle.

You must drive back to a central point to refuel before you run out.

Gameplay

  • Moon's Grasp is a 2D driving game where you tackle the rough bumpy terrain of the moon that is between you and the safepoint.

  • You have to reach the end of the level before you run out of oxygen.

  • One of the main feats of the game will be a risk mechanic that could determine that you reach the end quicker but at the expense of your oxygen.

  • You can use both the terrain which would feature sudden ramps, the low-gravity of the moon and your boosts to finish levels quicker at a risk.
